Algebra

Practice factorisation, equations, inequalities and functions. These topics appear regularly in examinations.

Functions

Understand linear, quadratic, exponential and logarithmic functions. Learn how graphs behave and how to interpret them.

Trigonometry

Learn identities, equations and applications of sine, cosine and tangent.

Calculus

Focus on differentiation, gradients and optimisation problems.

Probability

Understand events, outcomes and probability calculations.
